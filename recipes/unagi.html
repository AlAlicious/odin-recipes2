Unagi

Unadon or Grilled Eel Rice Bowl is a beloved Japanese dish consisting of steamed rice topped with grilled eels

that are glazed with a sweetened soy-based sauce (called tare) and caramelized, preferably over a charcoal fire.

Ingredients:

  • Unagi (Eel) fillets

  • Unagi sauce

  • Steamed rice

Steps:

  1. Prepare the homemade eel sauce

  2. Broil eel fillets and brush the eel sauce right before taking them out.

  3. Serve rice in a large rice bowl (donburi), brush with sauce, and serve eel fillets on top.
