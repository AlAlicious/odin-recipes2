Takoyaki

Takoyaki, a popular Japanese snack, are made with dashi-flavored batter with a crunchy outside and a soft,

fluffy inside that encases a chewy octopus surprise, all in a fun ball shape!

Ingredients:

    1 large egg

    1 ¼ cups water(300 mL)

    1 teaspoon dashi stock powder

    ¾ cup cake flour(100 g)

    2 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided

    3 ½ oz fresh boiled octopus(100 g), cut into 18 equal pieces

    2 tablespoons green onion, thinly sliced

    2 tablespoons red pickled ginger

    1 tablespoon tempura flakes
